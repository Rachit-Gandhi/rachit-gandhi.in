# About Me: Backend Brain, AI Curiosity, and Controlled Chaos

I’m Rachit — an engineer who enjoys building backend systems and applied AI workflows that survive contact with reality.

From my work and projects, a few themes keep repeating:
- I like turning messy workflows into reliable systems.
- I care about practical architecture over flashy complexity.
- I enjoy the intersection of LLM systems, data pipelines, and backend APIs.

## What I’m into right now

- **Backend architecture:** APIs, service boundaries, maintainability.
- **Applied AI systems:** retrieval, orchestration, evaluation, guardrails.
- **Developer experience:** tools that reduce friction and improve velocity.
- **Learning loops:** ship → observe → refactor → repeat.

## My engineering style (in one pass)

- Build fast, but keep structure.
- Prefer measurable outcomes over vague “improvements.”
- Use simple systems that are easy to operate.
- Keep refining until the workflow feels obvious.
